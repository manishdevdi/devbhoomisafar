About Us

Connecting souls to DevBhoomi's divine beauty

Who We Are

DevBhoomi Safar is not just a travel service — it’s a heartfelt journey into the soul of Uttarakhand. Founded by local explorers and spiritual enthusiasts, we are dedicated to crafting authentic experiences that connect travelers with the sacred, scenic, and cultural richness of the Himalayas. Whether it's a serene pilgrimage to Kedarnath or an offbeat trek through the Valley of Flowers, our team ensures every journey is safe, soulful, and unforgettable. We focus on sustainable travel that empowers local communities, supports native guides, and preserves the natural beauty and cultural heritage of DevBhoomi. With deep roots in Uttarakhand, we guide you not as strangers, but as family — welcoming you home.

Our Vision

To become India's most loved and trusted travel brand for spiritual and cultural exploration, inspiring individuals to reconnect with their roots and the divine landscapes of the Himalayas.

Our Mission

  • Provide authentic, soulful journeys to Uttarakhand's sacred and scenic destinations.
  • Empower local communities through tourism-driven opportunities and partnerships.
  • Ensure our tours are eco-conscious, safe, personalized, and memorable.

Why Choose Us

  • Local Expertise: Our team knows the terrain, culture, and hidden gems of Uttarakhand.
  • Spiritual Depth: Beyond sightseeing — we offer meaningful spiritual and healing experiences.
  • Tailored Itineraries: Customized trips for families, solo travelers, seniors, and adventure seekers.
  • 24x7 Support: Our support team is always just a call away during your journey.
