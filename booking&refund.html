Booking & Refund Policies

Booking Guidelines

Booking your spiritual journey with DevBhoomi Safar is simple and secure. Just select your tour, fill the booking form, and make your payment through our trusted gateway.

  • All bookings must be made via our website or verified agents.
  • 50% advance is required to confirm your spot.
  • Final payment must be completed 7 days prior to departure.
  • You'll receive a confirmation email and receipt after successful booking.

Payment Methods

  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.)
  • Debit/Credit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • Razorpay (secured payment gateway)
