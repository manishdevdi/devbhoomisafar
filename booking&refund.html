Booking & Refund Policy

We aim to ensure your travel booking is seamless and transparent. Below are our terms for booking, cancellation, and refund.

Booking Confirmation

  • All bookings must be confirmed via email or WhatsApp.
  • Advance payment is required to secure your reservation.
  • Final itinerary will be shared after booking confirmation.

Payment Terms

  • 50% advance is required at the time of booking.
  • Remaining 50% to be paid 1–2 days before journey start.
  • All payments are accepted via UPI, bank transfer, or online gateway.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation 7+ days before departure: 90% refund
  • Cancellation within 3–6 days: 50% refund
  • Cancellation within 48 hours: No refund
  • No-shows will be charged 100% of the tour cost

Refund Process

Refunds (if applicable) will be processed within 5–7 working days via the original mode of payment.

Force Majeure

DevBhoomi Safar is not liable for delays or cancellations caused by natural calamities, strikes, or government restrictions.

Contact

For any queries regarding your booking or refund, email us at devbhoomisafar@gmail.com
