Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Payments

How do I book a tour with DevBhoomi Safar?
You can easily book a tour by visiting our Booking Page. Fill in the required details, choose your tour, date, and number of travelers, and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email shortly.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking via our integrated Razorpay payment gateway. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Is my booking refundable?
We have a flexible cancellation policy. Cancellations made 15 days in advance are eligible for a 90% refund. Check our Booking Policy for full details.

Travel & Accommodation

What type of accommodation is provided?
We partner with reliable local guesthouses, hotels, and eco-stays that ensure both comfort and cultural authenticity. Room type depends on the package selected.
Are meals included in the package?
Yes, our standard packages include vegetarian meals during the journey. Special dietary requirements can be arranged if informed in advance.
Do you provide transport from major cities?
Yes. We offer pickup and drop services from cities like Haridwar, Rishikesh, and Dehradun. Additional charges may apply depending on distance.

Safety & Preparation

Are your tours safe for senior citizens?
Absolutely. We customize tours for seniors, ensuring slow-paced itineraries, medical support, and safe accommodations. Let us know the age and requirements during booking.
What should I pack for a Himalayan tour?
Warm clothing, trekking shoes, rain gear, personal meds, torch, power bank, and ID proof are essential. A detailed checklist will be shared after booking.
What happens in case of bad weather?
In case of natural disruptions, we either reschedule the itinerary or offer partial refunds. Your safety is our top priority and we stay in constant communication.

Tour & Package Queries

What is included in the tour package?
Our tours usually include transport, accommodation, guide, and meals. You can always refer to the itinerary of the specific tour you book for complete inclusion details.
Are meals provided?
Yes, most packages include breakfast and dinner prepared at local lodges or camps. Exact meal plans may vary, so kindly check the tour page.

Cancellation & Refund

Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, cancellations are allowed. However, cancellation terms vary depending on how early you inform us. Please review our Booking & Refund Policy for detailed information.
How long does a refund take?
Refunds are usually processed within 7 business days from the date of approval. The refund is credited to the original mode of payment.
